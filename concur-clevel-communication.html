01 · The Opportunity
Why Now

Our Current Reporting Has Gaps

🔒

Concur Reports Are Rigid

Out-of-the-box Concur views are limited to preconfigured layouts. There is no way to apply free-form filters, combine data across systems, or build views tailored to each business team's needs.

⚠ Limited Visibility
📋

Manual Exports Are Slow & Risky

Teams rely on manual spreadsheet exports that are time-consuming, error-prone, and outdated the moment they are shared. Decisions are made on stale data.

⚠ Data Latency Risk
🔀

No Cross-System Analysis

Expense data lives in isolation. Without a unified analytics layer, it is impossible to correlate spend with budget, project, or organizational context in a single view.

⚠ Siloed Data
💡

The opportunity: connect Concur's rich expense data to Power BI's analytical power — delivering interactive, always-current dashboards to every team, without replacing or disrupting the existing expense workflow.

03 · Business Benefits
Value Delivered

What This Means for the Business

🎯

Dashboards Tailored by Role

Each team gets a view built around their responsibilities — Finance sees the full picture, Cost Center owners see their own scope, management sees the consolidated summary. Automatic refresh means no spreadsheet exports, ever.

No more manual Excel pulls — dashboards are always current.
⚡

Faster Decisions, More Autonomy

Business teams no longer wait for Finance to produce a report. Self-service filters and drill-downs give every stakeholder the context they need to act — with richer data and greater independence.

From request to insight in seconds, not days.
