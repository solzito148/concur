Interactive expense dashboards — coming soon to Finance & business teams.

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce the release of our Concur Dashboard designed to provide greater visibility into users' expense data along our Cost Center areas.

What's Included in the New Reports?

The new reports allow users to review and analyze:

Expense Summary — high-level view of total spend, report volume, and average expense value for the selected period
Expense Type Statistics — spend and transaction count by expense type, including average value and share of total
Expense Overview by Year — year-over-year comparison of spend by period, with trend and variance against prior years
Expense Report Explorer — report-level detail with employee, status, submission and approval dates, and drill-down to the individual expense line
Expense by Account Assignment Object — spend allocated by cost center, project, WBS element, or internal order as posted to accounting objects
Amount Distribution — distribution of expenses across value ranges, highlighting concentration, thresholds, and outliers
Employee Spend Patterns — spend behavior by employee including volume, frequency, category mix, and comparison with peers
Vendor Concentration — ranked vendors by spend and transaction count, showing how concentrated spend is across suppliers
Benefits

These reports help managers and team members:

Get a shared starting point with headline numbers before drilling into detail
See which categories drive the spend and where policy limits or supplier negotiation would have the most impact
Investigate any report end to end — employee, status, approval dates, and line-level detail — without requesting a separate extraction
Connect T&E spend directly to budget owners and support cost-center-level accountability
Expose supplier dependency and highlight negotiation and consolidation opportunities
