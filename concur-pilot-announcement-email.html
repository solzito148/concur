Expense Analytics — Pilot

Interactive, role-based expense dashboards are coming to Finance & business teams.

📅 Pilot sessions begin the week of July 28, 2026 — invitations are on their way.

Dear Team,

We are pleased to announce the launch of the Concur + Power BI Expense Analytics pilot. Starting the week of July 28, selected Finance and business-unit users will have access to a new set of interactive Power BI dashboards connected directly to SAP Concur — giving everyone a faster, richer, and always up-to-date view of expense data.

What's changing

📊
Interactive dashboards
Explore expense data with free-form filters, drill-downs, and cross-dimensional views not available in standard Concur reports.
🔄
Automatic refresh, twice a day
Data is always current with no manual intervention — no more outdated spreadsheet exports.
🏢
Role-based views by Cost Center
Each team sees a view scoped to their own data, with the same access controls already in place in Concur.
📅
Period-over-period comparisons
Full history is preserved, enabling monthly, quarterly, and yearly trend analysis in a single report.
🔒
No change to the expense process
Concur remains the single source of data. The way you submit and approve expenses stays exactly the same.

How to be ready

Action required for pilot participants

If you have been selected for the pilot, please be ready for your scheduled session. No installation is required — the dashboards run directly in your browser via Power BI Service.

Attend your pilot session — an Outlook calendar invitation has already been sent with the link to the Power BI workspace and the session agenda.
Bring a real-world question — think of a cost or spend question you currently answer via a manual export. We will try to answer it live during the session.
