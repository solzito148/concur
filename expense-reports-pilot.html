Introduction 1 of 3
Why Are We Here?

Purpose of the
Initiative

AES processes thousands of expense reports each year across multiple business units, cost centers, and projects. Today this data is scattered across disconnected sources — hard to consolidate, slow to query, and difficult to act on.

This initiative builds a centralized, self-service Power BI analytics layer on top of the SAP Concur expense data, giving Finance, Operations, and Management the visibility they need — at any level of detail, in real time.

📊 Power BI 🔗 SAP Concur 🏢 Finance & Ops
Before  →  After
Fragmented Excel exports per BU → Unified Power BI dashboard
Days to produce a spend report → Real-time self-service analytics
No drill-down capability → Full transaction-level detail
Manual vendor / employee analysis → Automated DAX ranking & concentration
Static snapshots, no filters → Interactive slicers by date, AA, type
Target Users
Finance Controllers  ·  Cost Center Managers  ·  Project Leaders  ·  HR  ·  Internal Audit

Session Objectives
